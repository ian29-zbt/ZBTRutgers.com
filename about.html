About Beta Delta

A chapter defined by friendship, responsibility, academic support, service and connections that continue after graduation.

Who We Are

A Rutgers chapter with more than 75 years of history

Joe Glaser founded the Beta Delta Chapter in 1948. Since then, generations of brothers have shaped the chapter through leadership, academics, philanthropy, campus involvement and lifelong alumni relationships.

Today’s chapter combines that history with a period of significant growth: record recruitment, national recognition, strong academic performance and a new home at 106 College Avenue.
