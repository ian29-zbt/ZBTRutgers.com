The Experience

Most of brotherhood happens outside the scheduled calendar

A free afternoon in the backyard, a meal after class or a quick stop at the house can become the moments brothers remember most.

Travel, family events, athletics and work on the house bring together members who may not share classes or schedules. Those repeated experiences build relationships that continue after graduation.
